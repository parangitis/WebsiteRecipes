Slow Cooker Mac and Cheese

Description

This crockpot Buffalo chicken is great for hearty sandwiches that will please those who love Buffalo chicken wings.
This recipe is perfect for those days spent watching football. I like to top these with blue cheese or ranch dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Slow Cooker Mac
  • 1 cup of Cheese
  • 1 cup of Salt
  • 1 cup of Sour Cream
  • 1 cup of Pepper
  • 1 cup of Olive Oil
  • 1 cup of Flour
  • 1 cup of Water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the Slow Cooker Mac, Cheese, Salt, Sour Cream, Pepper, Olive Oil, Flour, and Water.
  3. Pour the mixture into a slow cooker.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  5. Remove the slow cooker from the oven.
