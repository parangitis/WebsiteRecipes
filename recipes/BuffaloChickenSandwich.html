Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Sandwiches

Description

This crockpot mac and cheese recipe is creamy, comforting, and takes just moments to assemble in a slow cooker.
Great for large family gatherings and to take to potluck dinners. It's always a big hit!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the butter, olive oil, cream, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the batter into a large baking dish.
  4. Pour the batter into the slow cooker.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours.
  6. Remove the slow cooker from the oven.
